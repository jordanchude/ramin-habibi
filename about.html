About Ramin Habibi

Ramin Habibi is a top-producing real estate advisor with over half a billion dollars in transactions to date. He launched his career working alongside two of the nation's most successful teams—The Robby Browne Team and The Carrie Chiang Team—where he developed the expertise to manage complex, high-stakes deals in New York City's most competitive market. During his tenure with Carrie Chiang, the team achieved #2 ranking for Medium Size Teams by Sales Volume in New York City and #5 in the Nation by The Wall Street Journal's REAL Trends Top 1,000 Agents, while also ranking #2 Sales Team across all of The Corcoran Group and earning the 2022 President's Council for exceeding $500M in annual sales.

Ramin comes from a top-producing real estate family operating in New York City, San Francisco/Silicon Valley, and Coastal Orange County. Together with his brothers, Roh and Obeid, they've built The Habibi Group—a nationwide referral network with over $1B in collective sales. A Bay Area transplant who moved to the Upper East Side in 2018, Ramin embodies the principle of selling what you live. As featured in The Observer, his deep neighborhood knowledge and proven track record make him an invaluable resource for buyers and sellers navigating Manhattan's luxury market.
